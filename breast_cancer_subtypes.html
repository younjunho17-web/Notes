01 · in situ carcinoma

Ductal Carcinoma In Situ

DCIS

Malignant cells confined within ducts by basement membrane — no metastatic capacity.

핵심 키워드
calcification comedo necrosis basement membrane intact no metastasis mammographic detection
Morphologic Subtypes (강의록)
  • Comedo: pleomorphic high-grade nuclei + central necrosis → 석회화
  • Cribriform: cookie-cutter–like round spaces + calcified secretory material
  • Micropapillary: bulbous protrusions — fibrovascular core 없음
  • Papillary: true papillae with fibrovascular cores — myoepithelial layer 없음
Molecular pathway
ER-positive pathway: BRCA2 germline → 1q gain / 16q loss → PIK3CA mutation → ADH → DCIS → Luminal cancer
HER2 pathway: TP53 mutation + HER2 amplification → DCIS (HER2+)
TNBC pathway: BRCA1 → TP53 → DCIS (ER−/HER2−)
Risk & 임상
Risk of invasive carcinoma: 8–10× (lifetime ~25–30%)

ADH와의 구분: size > 2 mm이면 DCIS로 분류

02 · in situ carcinoma

Lobular Carcinoma In Situ

LCIS

항상 incidental finding — 무증상, 석회화 없음, bilateral risk marker.

핵심 키워드
no calcification incidental E-cadherin loss bilateral (20–40%) multicentric ER+, PR+, HER2−
형태학적 특징
  • Monomorphic small, round, loosely cohesive cells
  • Oval or round nuclei, small nucleoli
  • Acini를 50% 미만 채울 때 ALH — 50% 이상 채울 때 LCIS
  • E-cadherin IHC: LCIS cells — 음성, 정상 luminal cells — 양성
  • Pagetoid spread: 인접 duct로 spreading
DCIS vs LCIS 비교
DCIS
  • Calcification 흔함
  • Palpable or mammographic
  • E-cadherin 양성
  • Surgical margin 중요
LCIS
  • Calcification 없음
  • 항상 incidental
  • E-cadherin 음성
  • Risk marker로 관리
Risk & 임상
8–10× (lifetime ~25–30%)

양측성 risk — ipsilateral & contralateral 모두 상승
수술적 절제가 risk를 낮추는지는 불명확 (강의록)

03 · invasive carcinoma · most common

Invasive Ductal Carcinoma (NST)

IDC / NST

"No special type" — invasive carcinoma의 대다수. Desmoplastic stroma + spiculated margin.

핵심 키워드
desmoplasia hard / spiculated ill-defined border firm to hard
Grade (Nottingham)
  • 3 criteria: tubule formation, nuclear pleomorphism, mitotic count
  • Grade 1: 5-yr survival ~85%
  • Grade 2: ~60%
  • Grade 3: ~15%
분자 분류
  • Luminal A: ER+, HER2−, low Ki-67
  • Luminal B: ER+, HER2−, high Ki-67 or HER2+
  • HER2: HER2+ (ER± )
  • TNBC: ER−, PR−, HER2−
Target therapy 연결 (강의록 Table 23.7)
  • ER+: tamoxifen, aromatase inhibitor, fulvestrant, CDK4/6i (palbociclib)
  • HER2+: trastuzumab (HERCEPTIN), TKI — 17q21 amplification 감지 by FISH
  • BRCA1/2: PARP inhibitor, platinum agents
  • TNBC: PD-L1 checkpoint inhibitor (under investigation)
Hazard pattern (강의록 그래프)
TNBC: early peak (~1.5 yr), rapid decline → early recurrence
HER2: bimodal peak (~2 yr, ~7 yr)
Luminal: flat, sustained long-term risk — late recurrence 주의

04 · invasive carcinoma · special type

Invasive Lobular Carcinoma

ILC

E-cadherin 소실 → dyscohesive → single-file infiltration — 촉진·영상 모두 어렵다.

핵심 키워드
single-file bilateral E-cadherin loss dyscohesive no desmoplasia (often) ER+, HER2−
형태학적 특징
  • Indian-file / targetoid pattern: stroma를 single file로 infiltrate
  • Desmoplastic response가 약해 palpation + imaging 모두 어려움
  • Intracytoplasmic lumina (signet ring–like)
  • CDH1 germline mutation → 유전성 lobular carcinoma (gastric signet ring도 동반)
LCIS와의 연속성
LCIS → ILC: 동일한 E-cadherin 소실 기전.
ILC는 LCIS와 함께 발견되는 경우 많음.
Bilateral & multicentric incidence 높음 — contralateral breast 주의.
IHC 패턴 (강의록 사진)
  • E-cadherin IHC: 음성 (반면 IDC는 양성)
  • ER: 핵 강양성
  • HER2: 대부분 음성

05 · clinical syndrome · T4d

Inflammatory Breast Carcinoma

Inflammatory

실제 염증이 아님 — dermal lymphatic obstruction에 의한 clinical presentation.

핵심 키워드
peau d'orange dermal lymphatics T4d staging erythema + edema no discrete mass
병태생리
  • 종양 세포가 dermal lymphovascular spaces를 막아 림프 울혈
  • 피부 edema → Cooper's ligament tethering → peau d'orange (orange-peel skin)
  • 실제 염증세포 침윤은 없거나 적음
  • 강의록: inflammatory breast cancer는 erythematous swollen breast에서 항상 고려
AJCC Staging (강의록 T4d)
T4d = Inflammatory carcinoma
T4a: chest wall extension
T4b: skin edema/ulceration, satellite nodules
T4c: both T4a + T4b
T4d: inflammatory carcinoma — 별도 카테고리
감별 / 임상 요점
  • Mastitis와 감별 중요 — antibiotic에 반응 없으면 biopsy
  • Rapidly progressive, poor prognosis
  • Skin punch biopsy로 dermal lymphatic tumor emboli 확인

06 · special type · TNBC

Carcinoma with Medullary Pattern

Medullary

BRCA1-associated TNBC — 역설적으로 dense lymphocytic infiltrate가 있어 softer on palpation.

핵심 키워드
lymphocytes pushing border BRCA1 TNBC softer mass pleomorphic nuclei
4대 형태학적 기준 (강의록)
  • Solid sheets of large cells — pleomorphic nuclei + prominent nucleoli
  • Frequent mitotic figures
  • Moderate to marked lymphoplasmacytic infiltrate (surrounding + within)
  • Pushing (non-infiltrative) border — 경계 밀어내는 형태
역설적 포인트
High-grade histology임에도 better prognosis than grade-matched IDC.

기전: dense TIL (tumor-infiltrating lymphocytes) → immune-mediated killing

BRCA1 inactivation → ER−/HER2− → TNBC pathway (강의록 Fig 23.14 blue arrow)
TNBC 임상 특성
  • Early metastasis hazard peak (~1.5 yr) then rapid decline
  • No hormonal or HER2 targeted therapy
  • Platinum agents + PARP inhibitor (BRCA1 mutation)
  • Checkpoint inhibitor (PD-L1) — under investigation

07 · special type · Luminal

Mucinous Carcinoma

Mucinous

Extracellular mucin pools에 떠 있는 tumor cell clusters — Luminal, favorable prognosis.

핵심 키워드
mucin pools ER+, HER2− Luminal gelatinous gross good prognosis older women
형태학적 특징
  • Large pools of extracellular mucin containing floating tumor cell clusters
  • Gross: gelatinous, well-circumscribed, soft
  • Pure type → very favorable; mixed type → intermediate
  • 강의록 Fig 23.22(B): mucinous background에 떠있는 세포군 확인
Luminal 계열 special types 비교
  • Mucinous: mucin pools, ER+, soft, good Px
  • Tubular: well-formed angulated tubules, excellent Px
  • Papillary: papillary structures, ER+
  • Lobular: E-cadherin−, single-file, ER+
강의록: Special types → 60% 30-year survival vs 20% no special type
Prognosis context
favorable

ER+ → hormonal therapy에 반응
Distant metastasis hazard: luminal curve는 flat, sustained — late recurrence 모니터링 필요

08 · special type · Luminal · best prognosis

Tubular Carcinoma

Tubular

Well-formed angulated tubules — 최고의 예후를 가진 invasive carcinoma.

핵심 키워드
well-formed tubules good prognosis ER+, HER2− angulated open lumens low grade
형태학적 특징
  • Well-differentiated angulated tubular structures with open lumens
  • Single layer of cells — myoepithelial layer 없음 (invasive이므로)
  • Low nuclear grade, minimal mitoses
  • >90% tubule formation → pure tubular; <90% → mixed
  • 강의록 Fig 23.22(C) 참조
vs. Sclerosing Adenosis 감별
Sclerosing adenosis: myoepithelial cells 있음, lobular architecture 유지, benign

Tubular carcinoma: myoepithelial cells 없음, irregular angulated tubules, invasive

IHC: p63 or smooth muscle actin → myoepithelial 감지
Prognosis
excellent — strongly correlated with favorable survival (강의록 Table 23.5)

Luminal A pathway, ER+ → tamoxifen / aromatase inhibitor
Lymph node 전이 적음

09 · nipple / in situ extension

Paget Disease of the Nipple

Paget

DCIS가 lactiferous sinus → nipple skin으로 basement membrane 미통과하며 확장.

핵심 키워드
eczematous nipple Paget cells in epidermis DCIS origin lactiferous sinus basement membrane intact oozing scaly crust
경로 (강의록 Fig 23.18)
  • DCIS (ductal system) → lactiferous sinus → nipple epidermis로 propagation
  • Basement membrane은 통과하지 않음 — in situ
  • Paget cells: large, pale, vacuolated cells within squamous epithelium
  • Disrupts tight squamous barrier → extracellular fluid seeps out → oozing scaly crust
임상적 포인트
  • 유두의 습진 모양 병변 (erythema, scaling, crusting)
  • 거의 항상 underlying DCIS or invasive carcinoma 동반
  • Paget disease of nipple ≠ underlying invasive carcinoma → staging은 underlying tumor 기준
  • AJCC 8th: Tis(Paget)로 분류 — 단, underlying tumor 있으면 그 tumor size 기준
감별 / IHC
  • Melanoma in situ, Toker cells와 감별
  • Paget cells: CK7+, HER2+, ER±
  • Melanoma: S100+, HMB45+
  • PAS+: intracytoplasmic mucin in Paget cells
